WELCOME TO THE BIKE CITY - DAVIS!

Davis, California, renowned for its bike-friendly streets and commitment to sustainability, is a vibrant community of 70,000 residents. Home to UC Davis, it blends academic excellence with a thriving arts scene and top-rated schools. Its extensive network of bike paths and lanes, coupled with green spaces like the UC Davis Arboretum, make it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. With a focus on community engagement and inclusivity, Davis offers a high quality of life in Northern California.
